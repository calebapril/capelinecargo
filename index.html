Our Services

WHAT WE DO

Capelinecargo currently posses one of the fastest growing logistics professionals in the industry. We specialize in both sea and freight, consolidation, packing, transport and logistics, warehousing and customs brokerage services. we are committed to making transportation of goods and services easier and faster. We provide adequate solution for your business, adapting to your specific needs and requirements as far as cargo transport is concerned.

Our freight department is equipped with all necessary structures to freight cargo in and out of the major hub station. Excellent air freight and tracking services across the world. Our cargo service is deployed to our clients according to the IATA standards. We bring simplicity and confidence to our customers.
